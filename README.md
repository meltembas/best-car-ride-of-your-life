## üì¶ About The Project
This isn't just a driving simulator; it's a test of chaos. I built this in Unity to experiment with vehicle dynamics and rigid body physics.

The premise is simple: you are a delivery truck on a lonely road filled with obstacles. You can drive carefully and weave through the crates... or you can plow right through them and watch the physics engine do its work.

**Core Features:**
* **Satisfying Physics:** Every crate and barrier reacts to impact. Smash them, tip them over, or push them off the road.
* **Low-Poly Aesthetic:** Clean, stylized visuals for a smooth experience.
* **Reactive Environment:** The world reacts to your driving style.
